Homeless Shelter

- Mary A Joseph

This dataset consists of data from a local homeless shelter provided in Kaggle on their homeless population seeking shelter with 121 people.
The visualization represents data of each client seeking shelter based on gender to understand the Female to Male ratio in order to analyse gender influences based on age, income and veteran status.
The two colours shown indicate veteran status of each client, with blue as No and Green as Yes.
The size of the circles indicate the number of nights spent at the shelter.

Age range with percentage


Age: This is the age of the client seeking shelter
Gender: This is the gender of the client seeking shelter
Veteran: This is an indicator to indicate if the client is a veteran or not
Income: This is the yearly income of the client seeking shelter
Nights: This is the number of nights the client seeking shelter has stayed at the shelter
Substance Abuse: This is an indicator to indicate if the client has substance abuse or not
Completed: This is an indicator to indicate if the client has completed shelters program
On Probation: This is an indicator to indicate if the client is on probation or not
